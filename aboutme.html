About me

My name is Xavannah Baars and I am a passionate creative with a love for design, technology and art. My portfolio reflects my style and way of working. As a CMD student, I have worked on different types of projects, ranging from interactive web applications to visual designs. Every project has a design process with its own story behind it.

How it started

During primary school I used to meet up with my friends to do crafts. We used to have two drawers in our office. We used one of the drawers for school supplies and the other we made into a crafts drawer. In that drawer were all kinds of things that we had made. Storage bins for erasers or a ladder where you could store your pens. The teachers saw that we were so passionate about it so they let us carry on with making stuff for in the drawer.

The journey

After primary school I went to high school. At high school I was only interested in two subjects. Those subjects were math and art. After high school I went to studie Marketing and Communication. This studie wasn’t completely what I was looking for. I did learn allot of things about the online presence of businesses and what is needed to ensure that your company is easier to find online. I took a year to decide what I wanted to do and then I found CMD. CMD (communication and multimedia design) is a studie at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam.
